Gear Up for Good
The Blue Ridge YMCA is proud to announce its upcoming gently
used sports equipment drive, Gear Up for Good. Let’s level the
playing field and help more kids play sports this year!
Bring your new or gently used sports equipment to the Blue
Ridge YMCA, Cedar Creek Family YMCA, or Harbor Point
Family YMCA.
Baseball Basketball Soccer
Tennis Swimming Golf
Yoga Surfing Boxing
Bikes Skateboards Cheerleading
* Note: We do not accept sports clothing or swimwear.
All donated sports equipment will be available for purchase at
the Blue Ridge YMCA. Community members are invited to shop
the sale for affordable, quality sports gear!
VOLUNTEER!
We are looking for volunteers to help organize donations and
prepare for the sale.
Volunteers must sign up on our official website.
