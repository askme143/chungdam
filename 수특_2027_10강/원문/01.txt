Movement in the Museum: Heart Centering Yoga
Find inner strength and peace in our Movement in the Museum:
Heart Centering Yoga session, where we practice the ancient art
of yoga surrounded by fossils.
Date and Time: Tuesday, May 19th from 5:45 pm to 6:30 pm
Location: Chesterfield Museum of Natural History
Classes
- Two separate classes (beginner and intermediate) will be held
at the same time — each led by a trained yoga instructor.
- The beginner class will include chairs for those who need them.
Participation Fees
- Museum Members: $5
- Early Registration (Non-Members): $15
- Day of Registration (Non-Members): $20
General Information
- Participants must be 18 years or older and will be required to
sign a participation agreement.
- Please bring your yoga mat and water as they are not provided.
- Register for the class online.
- Parking in the museum is free after 5:00 pm.
Contact Information
If you have any questions, contact Grace Harrell (Education
Outreach Coordinator, Chesterfield Museum of Natural History)
by calling 305-349-6483.
