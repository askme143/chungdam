2026 Family Green Day
Come to the 2026 Family Green Day and enjoy seasonal,
environmentally-themed activities!
Date: Tuesday, April 7
Time: 9:00 AM and 1:30 PM
* Each session lasts 2 hours and 30 minutes.
Please select only one when booking.
Location: Avioly Environmental Center
Tickets
- Children aged 3 and over: $25
- Children under 3: $12
Activities
1. Making a toy animal with various kinds of leaves and pinecones
2. Face painting with nature-themed designs such as butterflies
and flowers
Other Information
- Aimed primarily at 3-11 year olds, this event is enjoyable for
the whole family!
Frequently Asked Questions
1. Do accompanying adults need tickets?
- No! Adults can enter free with the purchase of a child’s ticket.
2. Is there parking on site?
- Yes, paid parking is available.
