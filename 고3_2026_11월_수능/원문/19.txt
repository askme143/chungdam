“Where could it be?” Sophie asked herself.
It had been more than ten years since she had last visited
the area where she had grown up.
The village had changed a lot over time.
Uncertain, she awkwardly looked around at her surroundings.
She walked the narrow streets of the village, unsure about
which way to go.
Suddenly, Sophie saw a familiar sight.
“Yes, this must be it,” she thought.
In front of her was a wall with flowers painted on it.
Although the colors were now faded, the familiar shapes
on the wall were the same ones she had painted with her
father as a child.
Sophie nodded, smiled brightly, and walked toward the
gate.
At last, she had finally found the house she had grown up in.
