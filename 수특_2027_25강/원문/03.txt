 Once learned and after a few hours of practice, the
required actions for driving become second nature, transferable
to almost any vehicle and performed without explicit thinking.
 Information appears to be perceived directly; the
affordances of the road, the car, and the traffic implicitly
detected. Actually, this sort of automatization of conscious learning can
be jeopardized by the return of explicit awareness.
