The shoreline was known for having the best fishing spots on
the lake. Jessie was sitting at one of those spots, but her fishing
line still hadn’t moved an inch. With a deep sigh, Jessie pulled
out the line and cast it back into the water.
Her dream of catching a big fish was fading. “I can’t believe
I haven’t caught a fish yet. Not a single one,” she thought.
Just as she was about to give up and leave, the fishing line
suddenly became tense. Jessie excitedly held onto the fishing
pole as it began to move around wildly. Her eyes widened and
her heart began to beat faster with excitement. With a big smile,
she could feel that this was the biggest fish she had ever
caught!
